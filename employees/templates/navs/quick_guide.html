Getting Started - A Quick Guide

WorkforceHub empowers you to manage your team efficiently and effortlessly. Here's a quick guide to get you up and running:

  1. Sign Up for a Free Account:

    The journey begins with a free WorkforceHub account. Head over to our signup page and enter your details (valid email and secure password). It's a quick process, and you'll be ready to personalize your experience in no time.

  2. Create Your Profile:

    Once signed up, take a moment to personalize your profile. Add a profile picture (optional) and ensure your contact information is accurate.

  3. Build Your Organization:

    Now comes the exciting part - building your organization! WorkforceHub allows you to create a dedicated workspace for your team. Simply name your organization and fill in any relevant details.

  4. Establish Your Branch:

    For larger organizations, creating branches within your organization provides a structured approach. This allows you to manage geographically dispersed teams or departments separately. Each branch can have its own set of admins and employees.

  5. Delegate with Confidence:

    Empower your team by creating delegate admins. This can be another team member or even yourself with a different email and username. Delegate admins can oversee specific branches, allowing for shared responsibility and streamlined management.

  6. Log In and Take Control:

    Once everything is set up, simply log in to your WorkforceHub account and select your branch (if applicable). From here, you'll have access to a comprehensive dashboard where you can manage employee schedules, track time, process payroll, and more.
